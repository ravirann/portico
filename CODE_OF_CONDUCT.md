# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making Portico a welcoming, respectful, and productive open-source community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for impact.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect maintainers' time and project boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, threats, spam, and deliberately disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainers through the security/contact channel listed in this repository.
