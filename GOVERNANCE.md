# Governance

Portico is maintained as an open-source project with a bias toward practical engineering, reliability, and security.

## Project principles

1. Reliability beats cleverness.
2. Deterministic automation should be preferred over opaque agent behavior.
3. AI should be observable, constrained, and useful.
4. Security and tenant isolation are core product requirements.
5. Operators need evidence, not just status messages.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests.
- Triaging issues and discussions.
- Defining project direction and release priorities.
- Enforcing security and community standards.
- Keeping the public roadmap understandable.

## Decision making

Most decisions happen through issues and pull requests. For large changes, open a proposal issue first and describe:

- The problem.
- The proposed solution.
- Alternatives considered.
- Operational and security implications.
- Migration impact.

## Becoming a maintainer

Regular contributors may be invited as maintainers after demonstrating high-quality contributions, good judgment, and care for the community.
